    We’re All Going To Die is a top down cooperative twin stick shooter in which the players face off against waves of enemies. Fight against different kinds of bosses, that can be more ranged or melee oriented, all with their own special moves and abilities.

    Responsibilities

    • - Communicated and collaborated with the team and the leads to improve documentations, gameplay and features
    • - Used Jira to manage tasks and responsibilties level focusing on one mental health condition, within a week
    • - Created multiple prototypes and tested gameplay and player interactions at every point
    • - Used FMOD to add the background music and sound to the levels
    • - Used multiplayer replication to ease the process of synchronizing data and procedure calls, between clients and servers, using Unreal Engine blueprints

    Features and Mechanics

    • - City level: Escalator systems were added to all four corners of the outpost to provide players an advantage in the event they get stuck. To save the stuck player, the escalators can be lowered.
    • - Skybridge level: Rotating bridges were added to provide players with true freedom of movement. Bridges rotate at random intervals but connect to all platforms, thus opening up alternate paths at all times.
    • - Special level: Healbot mechanic was added in order to heal the player. The healbot will follow around the player when the health goes below 99.99%. Otherwise, it will patrol around the map randomly. For the healbot to follow and heal them, the players will have to be in its range. Once the character has 100% health, the healbot will stop following and will go back to patrolling.
