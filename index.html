About Me

Hi, I'm Pranjall—a passionate game designer and creative technologist who believes that the fusion of art and technology can transport us to entirely new realms. With Unreal Engine as my primary tool, I craft immersive digital experiences where every project is a journey into uncharted territories of interactivity and storytelling.


My career has been an evolving adventure, one that continuously explores the seamless blend of innovative game design with the precision of software engineering. I thrive on transforming bold ideas into interactive realities—whether it's designing captivating gameplay mechanics or building expansive virtual worlds. Every challenge is an opportunity to push the boundaries of digital storytelling and redefine what it means to engage with a game.
